Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of the India national team. He is one of the most successful batsmen in modern cricket.

Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is a former Indian cricketer and one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He is the highest run-scorer in international cricket and has numerous records to his name. Tendulkar's career spanned over two decades, and he is widely regarded as a cricketing legend.

Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats. He is a right-handed batsman and is known for his aggressive batting style. Sharma has been a key player for India in all formats of the game and has achieved numerous records throughout his career. He is also the vice-captain of the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
